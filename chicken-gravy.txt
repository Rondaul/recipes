This is the recipe to make chicken gravy.

1. Put chilli masala, garam masala, meat masala to the chicken.
2. Mix the masala with salt.
3. Put oil in the pan.
4. Put sliced onion and chilli to the pan.
5. Fry it for 5 min.
6. Put the chicken in the pan.
6. Put some water after 10 min of frying.
8. Cook it for 20-30 min.
9. You have your chicken gravy.
